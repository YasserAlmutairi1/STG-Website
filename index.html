ABOUT STG STUDIOS

Who We Are

STG Studios is an independent game development studio dedicated to crafting immersive and thrilling 3D gaming experiences. Our mission is to bring creativity, innovation, and fun to every project we undertake.

Our Journey

Founded in 2023 as Gops Games, STG Studios initially explored multiplayer games. While no multiplayer titles were released at the time, this period was crucial for building the foundation of creativity, technical expertise, and vision that drives our studio today.

Our First Release

Our debut official game, The Cowboy Adventure, is a 2D platformer with a classic cowboy theme. The Alpha version launched on July 23, 2025, and was later delisted on August 26, 2025, marking an important milestone in our ongoing journey of growth and innovation.
